About This Site

This website is dedicated to spreading authentic teachings based on the Qur'an and Sunnah as understood by the companions and early generations.

We share content from senior scholars like Shaykh al-Fawzan, Shaykh Rabee', Shaykh Uthaymeen, Shaykh Bin Baz and others.

Basic Aqeedah Principles

  • ✅ Tawheed: Allah is One with no partners in His Lordship, Worship, or Names & Attributes.
  • ✅ Following the Qur'an and authentic Sunnah with the understanding of the Sahabah.
  • ✅ Rejecting bid’ah (innovations) in belief and worship.
  • ✅ Allegiance to Ahl al-Sunnah and separation from Ahl al-Bid’ah.
